About Us

The predecessor of the present MY FUture Unversity was the former Mini_ Water Technology Institute, which was established in 1986 with the objective of producing trained professionals in the field of civil and water resources. After its transformation to My Future University, since June 2012, it is undergoing into a radical transformation having a vision to be one of the best universities in the country and a center of Excellence in the water sector in Eastern Oakland.
